Welcome to the BEA Visualizer

The purpose of this application is to quickly view information from the Bureau of Economic Analysis

Controls

In order to keep the UI clear, much of the controls are in the keys

  • Keys 1,2,3 change the order of the data by standard, numerical, and alphabetical
  • Pressing the number again reverses the data
  • shift-up and shift-down change the year by 1, shift-pgUp and shift-pgDn change the year by 10
